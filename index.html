Mapping the path to a sustainable future

The Future Map is a free, open resource dedicated to informing, enabling, and inspiring action on clean energy, climate change, and the environment, from global policy to individual impact.

Mission

To inform, enable, and inspire a sustainable future.

The Future Map exists because the climate crisis is not a distant problem, it's happening now, at an alarming pace. Our future demands understanding and action. This map is being built to show you: where things stand, what is being done, who is doing it, and how you can be part of the change.

The Foundations

Inform

Clear, accurate, and regularly updated information on clean energy development and climate impact across every region of the world.

Enable

Resources, tools, and connections to organizations working on the ground, so anyone can turn understanding to meaningful action.

Inspire

Stories of progress, remarkable people, and the case that the future is not set in stone, and that action at every scale makes a difference.
